Learn More About Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is an open-world action RPG developed by HoYoverse. Players explore the fantasy world of Teyvat while uncovering secrets, battling enemies, and forming teams of powerful characters.

The World of Teyvat

Teyvat is divided into several nations, each inspired by real-world cultures and governed by an Archon who represents an element. These gods shape the ideals and traditions of their people.

Nation Element Archon

Elemental System

Combat in Genshin Impact revolves around elements. Combining elements triggers powerful reactions that deal extra damage or provide effects.

Element Description
Pyro Fire-based attacks that deal high damage
Hydro Water element that enables reactions
Electro Lightning element focused on energy
Cryo Ice element that freezes enemies
Anemo Wind element that spreads reactions
Geo Earth element focused on defense
Dendro Nature element related to growth

Gameplay Overview

Players form a party of four characters and switch between them during combat. Exploration, quests, puzzles, and boss battles are key parts of the gameplay experience.

  • Open-world exploration
  • Story and side quests
  • Boss fights and domains
  • Team building and strategy
